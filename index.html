About Butter & Salt

Welcome to Butter & Salt, your trusted partner for exceptional catering services in Indonesia. We are a passionate small company dedicated to bringing delicious, fresh, and affordable food to schools, homeowners, and food lovers everywhere.


From our signature fast food offerings to our comprehensive buffet services (jasa prasmanan), we pride ourselves on using quality ingredients and creating memorable dining experiences. Our team understands that great food brings people together, and we're here to make your events special.


Whether you're planning a school event, home gathering, or corporate function, Butter & Salt delivers taste, quality, and service that exceeds expectations.
